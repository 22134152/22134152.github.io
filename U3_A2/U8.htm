What I did in unit 8

In this unit we learnt lots about computing power with smart phones and other mobile devices, we also learnt about what you can do with this computing power including downloading apps and what purposes different apps have.
The main part of this unit was that we needed to design our own app using MIT App Inventor and a client's description. In preparation for designing an app I had a in-depth look at different created apps to see what their purpose was along with any potential designs that I could get inspiration from.
