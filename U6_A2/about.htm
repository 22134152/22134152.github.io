About us

Celtec Games is a game company originating in north-wales as a game store before slowly expanding into creating our own games and creating popular games played around the world. Our first game that we created was shatterblade. Since developing ths game it has now became one of the most popular story games that you can find. Shatterblade is an incredible game that showed us that we should create more

After Shatterblade was a success that inspired us to create new games eldertide. A game created around the concept of shatterblade, improving on the storyline and characters. Eldertide turned out to be just as successful being just as popular as shatterblade and inspiring us to create many more games including the titanfall series and many more story games like the terraria series. All of these games have been enjoyed across the world.

Celtec has grown massively since being created as we never expected that would be designing our own games and instead just staying as a store based across the UK. We have lots of new plans for the future of the company creating lots of new games.
